Learn to play The Royal Game of Ur

Goal

Get all of your pieces around and off the board!

How to Play

1. Roll the Dice.

Click on your dice to roll them.

The value shown is how far you can move one of your pieces!


2. Move a Piece.

On desktop, click a piece to move it.

On mobile, you can drag or double tap the piece you wish to move.


Rosette Tiles

Extra Moves:

Moving your tiles onto rosette tiles gives you another move!


Safe:

Pieces on rosette tiles cannot be taken!
